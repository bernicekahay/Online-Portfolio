Resume
I supported the Words With Friends IP, leading various in-game events and features from conception through production for millions of players to enjoy. I also lead the UX team through the tech launch of an unannounced project, built the team’s foundational UI/UX implementation pipeline, trained my team onto Unity through workshops, documented best practices and guidelines, and enabled cross collaboration between Engineers, Technical Artist, and UX that were pivotal to the project’s success. Please check out some of my work below. More information is available on request.
Live Content & Events
Throughout my time at Zynga, I also supported the design and launch of various in-game content and events on Words With Friends.
Leadership
In addition to owning feature designs, I lead the UX team to transition over to a completely new project. I built up the foundational workflow and processes for UX to collaborate with Technical Artists and Engineers to successfully implement UI. I trained the UX team onto Unity, mentored other UX designers, and bridged gaps in knowledge and communication between UX and partner teams.

In 2023, I co-lead zPride, Zynga’s LGBTQ+ employee resource group with 370+ members, and managed our 12-member leadership board to organize Zynga's largest PlayWithPride celebrations across 15 Zynga studios.
Most of the UX team had no experience with Unity or had not interacted with the game engine in years. As the designer with the most Unity experience on our project, I developed an onboarding strategy to enable the UX team a succesfull transition.

I created onboarding documents, centralized educational team resources, and hosted a 5 day workshop to cover basic topics, team workflows, and fundamental technical knowledge. By the end of the training, UX Designers with no prior knowledge of Unity could comfortably start implementing UI.
When I joined the unannounced project, the UXD process was still new and uncharted. I improved the project's foundational workflow by establishing daily syncs, smoothening the PR process, centralizing UX resources, and bridging disciplines.

Throughout the project, I mentored other UX Designers and guided them in best practices, design uniformity, technical skills, and confidence in communication. I continued to lead the adaption of the UX process as the team’s needs and environment evolved.
I co-lead zPride, Zynga’s LGBTQ+ Employee Resource Group, where I oversaw a leadership board of 12 members and a community of 370+ members. During my time as co-lead, I organized zPride’s roadmap, budget, external partnerships, internal communications, and successfully launched Zynga’s largest PlayWithPride celebration ever.

The 2023 PlayWitPride celebration featured 15 Zynga game studio activations, spotlights, social events, and a first-ever permanent PlayWithPride website. To learn more, please check out zynga.com/playwithpride.
